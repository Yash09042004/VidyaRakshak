How To Use?

Step 1: Before starting, make sure that you are logged in successfully. Click on the "Create New Exam" button.

Step 2: Now, Rename your exam.

Step 3: You will see an interface like this.

Step 4: Now set the time and date and modify settings/options as per your requirement

Step 5: Now, Click on the "Upload Questions JSON".

Step 6: Select your "questions.json" file

Step 7: Once your file is successfully uploaded, click on the "Generate Random Quiz" button and Check for the pop-up of "Random quiz generated successfully". Once done

Step 8: Now click on the edit button as shown.

Step 9: Once you do changes (if any) click on the "Save" button. Once you save all the questions

Step 10: Once you create quiz successfully, just open the exam and copy the link of that exam from search bar and share it with your students!
